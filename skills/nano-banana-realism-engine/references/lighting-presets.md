# Lighting Presets

Select one main lighting pattern per concept. Tie lighting to the setting and camera choice.

## Natural window light
Default for interiors, products in homes, kitchens, bathrooms, offices, and calm lifestyle scenes.

- Light quality: soft directional daylight from one side
- Shadow behavior: gentle, believable falloff
- Best for:
  - interiors
  - skincare, homewares, food, artisan products
  - calm portraiture
- Useful language:
  - `soft north-facing window light, gentle shadow falloff, natural room bounce, believable ambient fill`

## Golden hour exterior
Use for warmth, aspiration, family moments, commuting, travel, outdoor services, and emotionally positive ad imagery.

- Light quality: warm low-angle sun
- Shadow behavior: long but soft enough to stay believable
- Best for:
  - aspirational outdoor scenes
  - family and mobility
  - taxi or vehicle lifestyle images
- Useful language:
  - `late golden hour sunlight, warm edge light, natural atmospheric haze, realistic outdoor contrast`

## Indoor ambient practicals
Use when the image should feel like a normal evening or lived-in home.

- Light quality: mixed practical lamps and room light
- White balance: slightly warm and imperfect is acceptable
- Best for:
  - home service ads
  - evening interiors
  - UGC and documentary scenes
- Useful language:
  - `ambient household lighting with practical lamps, slightly uneven illumination, realistic mixed color temperature`

## Overcast daylight
Use for soft realism without dramatic highlights, especially for exteriors, storefronts, loading scenes, and working-day documentation.

- Light quality: broad and diffuse
- Best for:
  - moving scenes
  - delivery scenes
  - product-in-use outside
  - documentary realism
- Useful language:
  - `soft overcast daylight, diffuse shadows, naturally muted contrast, realistic cloud-filtered light`

## Diffused softbox commercial
Use sparingly when a cleaner product ad is required but must still look human-made.

- Light quality: broad diffused key, gentle fill, realistic reflections
- Guardrails:
  - keep surfaces imperfect
  - retain subtle shadow edges
  - do not sterilize the scene
- Useful language:
  - `large diffused key light with subtle fill, realistic material reflections, restrained commercial polish`
