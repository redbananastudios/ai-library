# UGC & Avatar Guide - Higgsfield Video

## Overview

Higgsfield provides three distinct UGC workflows for creating authentic, user-generated-style content and talking-head videos:

1. **UGC Builder** (Veo 3.1) — Photorealistic talking heads with lip-sync and emotion control
2. **UGC Factory** — Batch ad production using 40+ pre-built templates
3. **Avatar Speech Tool** — Generate avatars with natural voice integration

---

## UGC Builder (Veo 3.1)

### What It Is

Veo 3.1 generates photorealistic talking-head videos with native lip-sync. Ideal for testimonials, product demos, educational videos, and avatar-based ads. Supports emotion control and voice integration.

### Access

```
Navigate to https://higgsfield.ai/ugc
```

### Platform: UGC Builder Interface

The UGC Builder has three input sections:

#### 1. Script / Dialogue Input
Paste the full script or dialogue that the avatar will speak:
```
"Hi, I'm excited to tell you about this amazing product. It's perfect for anyone who wants quality and style..."
```

- **Max length:** Up to 2–3 minutes of dialogue (15–30s video typically)
- **Supports:** Natural speech patterns, pauses (use "..." for hesitation), emphasis (use *asterisks* or **bold** for tone cues)
- **Language:** English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Chinese, Japanese, Korean (others on request)

#### 2. Motion Tone Selection
Choose how the avatar moves and gestures:

| Tone | Vibe | Use Case |
|------|------|----------|
| **Casual** | Natural, relaxed hand gestures, conversational | Social media, testimonials, influencer style |
| **Testimonial** | Professional yet warm, direct eye contact, genuine | Case studies, customer reviews, trust-building |
| **Cinematic** | Polished, subtle movement, broadcast-quality | Brand films, premium products, luxury goods |
| **Dynamic** | High energy, expressive gestures, engaging | Product launches, hype videos, motivational |
| **Narrator** | Controlled, measured delivery, authoritative | Educational, instructional, webinars |
| **Enthusiastic** | Upbeat, positive energy, celebratory | Announcements, celebrations, excitement-driven |

#### 3. Avatar/Voice Configuration

**Avatar Selection:**
- **Default (photorealistic human)** — standard appearance, customizable via description
- **Custom avatar** — upload reference image or describe appearance

**Voice Options:**
- **Auto-generate** — Higgsfield generates natural voice matching motion tone and language
- **Upload audio** — Use pre-recorded voice or text-to-speech (must match script length)
- **Voice characteristics** — Specify accent, age, gender, tone if using auto-generate

### Step-by-Step: UGC Builder Workflow

#### Step 1: Navigate to UGC Builder
```
Go to https://higgsfield.ai/ugc
```

#### Step 2: Paste Script
In the "Dialogue" field, paste the full script the avatar will speak:
```
"Thank you for watching. I'm here to show you how easy it is to use our platform.
Whether you're a beginner or expert, you'll be creating in minutes."
```

#### Step 3: Choose Motion Tone
Select from 6 motion tones based on the vibe you want (Casual, Testimonial, Cinematic, Dynamic, Narrator, Enthusiastic).

#### Step 4: Configure Avatar
- **Avatar style:** Default (photorealistic) or Custom
- **Voice:** Auto-generate or upload
- **Duration:** Auto-calculated from script length (typically 5–30s)

#### Step 5: Configure Video Settings
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 (portrait, social), 16:9 (landscape, broadcast), 1:1 (square)
- **Resolution:** 1080p or 4K
- **Background:** Simple (white/neutral), Branded (custom color/logo), or Scenery (AI-generated environment)

#### Step 6: Generate
Click **Generate**. UGC Builder will:
1. Synthesize voice with natural prosody and emotion matching the motion tone
2. Generate photorealistic avatar with lip-sync
3. Animate gesture and posture matching the script emotion
4. Render video in selected format

**Generation time:** 30–60s depending on script length and resolution.

#### Step 7: Download
Once complete, click **Download** to save video to Downloads folder.

## UGC Factory (Batch Production)

### What It Is

UGC Factory is Higgsfield's **batch ad production system** with 40+ pre-built templates for common ad scenarios. Upload a script, select a template, and UGC Factory generates multiple variations automatically.

### Access

```
Navigate to https://higgsfield.ai/flow/builder
```

### Available Templates (40+)

#### E-commerce / Product (12 templates)
- Product unboxing
- Product showcase (rotating)
- Before/after transformation
- Feature breakdown (3-step)
- Price reveal / special offer
- Customer testimonial (single avatar)
- Product demo (hands-on, desktop)

#### SaaS / Software (10 templates)
- Dashboard walkthrough
- Feature highlight
- Use-case scenario
- Onboarding tutorial (5-step)
- Problem/solution

#### Services / Consulting (8 templates)
- Service explainer
- Case study narrative
- Client success story
- Team introduction
- Service pricing (comparison)

#### Lifestyle / Brand (10 templates)
- Brand story / heritage
- Lifestyle montage
- Community testimonial
- Behind-the-scenes (BTS)
- Product lifestyle (aspirational)

### Step-by-Step: UGC Factory Workflow

#### Step 1: Navigate to UGC Factory
```
Go to https://higgsfield.ai/flow/builder
```

#### Step 2: Choose Template
Browse 40+ templates organized by industry. Select one matching your ad type (e.g., "Product Demo – 30s").

#### Step 3: Fill in Script
Each template has pre-defined "scenes" with placeholders:
```
Scene 1: [HERO_STATEMENT] — e.g., "Introducing the fastest notebook app"
Scene 2: [FEATURE_HIGHLIGHT_1] — e.g., "Instant sync across all devices"
Scene 3: [FEATURE_HIGHLIGHT_2] — e.g., "Beautiful, distraction-free interface"
Scene 4: [CTA] — e.g., "Download now for free"
```

Fill each placeholder with your script content.

#### Step 4: Configure Voice & Avatar
- **Voice tone:** Professional, Casual, Enthusiastic, Instructional
- **Language:** English, Spanish, French, German, etc.
- **Avatar:** Default (photorealistic) or Custom (upload reference)
- **Music:** Select from stock music library or upload custom track

#### Step 5: Configure Variations
- **Generate N variations:** 1, 3, 5, or 10 versions with minor differences (different avatars, voice tones, music)
- **Background options:** Branded (logo, color), Scenery (AI-generated), or Minimal (white/neutral)

#### Step 6: Generate All
Click **Generate All**. UGC Factory will:
1. Generate all scenes using Kling 3.0 or equivalent high-quality model
2. Synthesize voice(s) with natural prosody
3. Composite background music
4. Output all variations (e.g., 3 variations × 30s each)

**Generation time:** 2–5 minutes for 3 variations (depending on total duration).

#### Step 7: Download Variations
Once complete, download all variations as a ZIP file. Each variation is labeled: `ad-variation-01.mp4`, `ad-variation-02.mp4`, etc.

#### Step 8: A/B Test
Upload variations to your ad platform (Facebook Ads, TikTok Ads, Google Ads) and run A/B test to see which resonates.

## Avatar Speech Tool

### What It Is

Avatar Speech is a specialized tool for creating avatars that speak using voice control. Useful for creating multiple avatar variations with pre-recorded or synthesized voice.

### Access

```
Embedded in https://higgsfield.ai/ugc or callable via Veo 3.1 API
```

### Configuration

**Avatar Setup:**
- **Body pose:** Standing, seated, upper-body-only
- **Clothing:** Custom description or reference image
- **Environment:** Studio (neutral), Office, Coffee shop, Outdoor, Custom

**Voice Input:**
- **Pre-recorded audio:** Upload MP3 or WAV file (must be clear, well-balanced)
- **Text-to-speech:** Paste script, select voice (100+ voices available), customize pitch/speed
- **Voice cloning:** Upload 30s sample of a specific voice to create a custom voice model

**Emotion / Expression:**
- **Base emotion:** Neutral, Happy, Sad, Serious, Thoughtful
- **Micro-expressions:** Subtle eye contact, head nods, hand gestures (controlled per 5s interval)
- **Gaze direction:** Camera (eye contact), Left, Right, Down (thinking)

### Step-by-Step: Avatar Speech Workflow

#### Step 1: Create / Import Avatar
- **Create new:** Describe appearance (gender, age, clothing, ethnicity)
- **Import reference:** Upload image or describe person

#### Step 2: Set Pose & Environment
Choose body pose (standing/seated) and background environment.

#### Step 3: Input Voice
- **Option A:** Upload audio file (MP3, WAV)
- **Option B:** Paste script → select voice from library → customize speed/pitch
- **Option C:** Upload voice sample → train custom voice model (takes ~5 min)

#### Step 4: Configure Emotions
- Set base emotion (neutral, happy, serious, etc.)
- Add micro-expressions every 5 seconds (eye contact, nod, hand gesture)

#### Step 5: Generate
Click **Generate**. Avatar Speech will:
1. Lip-sync avatar to audio
2. Apply micro-expressions and emotion
3. Render video in selected format

**Generation time:** 15–45s depending on video length.

#### Step 6: Download & Use
Download video in 9:16, 16:9, or 1:1 format. Ready for social, ads, or integration.

## UGC Best Practices

### Script Writing
- **Keep it conversational:** Write like you're talking to a friend, not reading a memo
- **Emotional hooks first:** Open with benefit/curiosity, then explain features
- **Natural pauses:** Use "..." for hesitation or thinking
- **Call-to-action last:** End with clear action (download, visit, buy)
- **Target length:** 8–15s for social, 15–30s for ads, 30–60s for testimonials/educational

### Motion Tone Selection
- **Testimonial** for reviews, case studies, trust-building
- **Casual** for social media, influencer content
- **Cinematic** for premium/luxury brands
- **Dynamic** for launches, announcements, energy-driven products
- **Narrator** for education, instructions, tutorials

### Avatar Customization
- **Match brand aesthetic:** If using custom avatar, ensure clothing/appearance aligns with brand
- **Diversity:** Use varied avatars across campaign variations for broader appeal
- **Consistency:** If creating multiple videos, use same avatar to build recognition

### Voice Selection
- **Match tone:** Testimonial tone needs warm, genuine voice; product demo needs clear, authoritative
- **Language:** Auto-generate voice in same language as script for natural lip-sync
- **Accent:** Specify if campaign targets specific region

### Testing & Iteration
- **Generate variations:** Always create 3+ variations with different avatars, voices, music
- **A/B test:** Run on platform (Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn) for 48–72 hours before scaling
- **Metrics:** Track CTR, engagement rate, conversion rate to identify winning variation
- **Iterate:** Double down on winning formula, adjust losing variations

## Performance Notes

### Generation Speed
- **UGC Builder (single script):** 30–60s
- **UGC Factory (3 variations, 30s each):** 2–5 minutes
- **Avatar Speech (15s clip):** 15–45s

### Quality Targets
- **Lip-sync accuracy:** 95%+
- **Eye contact:** Natural, not robotic
- **Hand gestures:** Contextual to script (product-related gestures on product mention)
- **Voice prosody:** Natural inflection, matching motion tone
- **Background quality:** Clean, professional, distraction-free

### Limitations & Workarounds

| Limitation | Workaround |
|-----------|-----------|
| Script max ~3 minutes | Split longer videos into 2–3 shorter clips, stitch together |
| Limited custom avatars | Use UGC Builder's "Custom" option to upload reference image |
| Voice accent limited | Use pre-recorded audio if specific accent needed; auto-gen covers major languages |
| Hand gestures generic | For precise gestures, use Cinema Studio + Kling for full-body control |

## Integration with Other Workflows

### UGC Builder → Kling 3.0
Use UGC Builder for talking head (avatar) sections, then integrate full-body action via Kling 3.0:
1. Generate avatar talking head in UGC Builder (9:16, 10s)
2. Generate action/product scenes in Kling 3.0 (9:16, 5–10s each)
3. Stitch in order: intro (avatar) → product action → outro (avatar)

### UGC Factory → Cinema Studio
Use UGC Factory for ad copy/concept, then upgrade to Cinema Studio for cinematic hero shots:
1. Generate UGC Factory variations (test on platform)
2. Winning concept → re-shoot with Cinema Studio camera body/lens simulation
3. Composite Cinema Studio shots with UGC Factory dialogue/music

### Avatar Speech → Podcast / Voiceover
Use Avatar Speech to create visual voice-overs for podcasts:
1. Record/synthesize podcast audio
2. Create avatar that matches host personality
3. Generate avatar video in 16:9
4. Composite podcast audio with avatar video for YouTube/LinkedIn
