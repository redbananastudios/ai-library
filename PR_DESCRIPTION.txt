### Skill: higgsfield-video (v1.0.0)
- **9 Video Models**: Cinema Studio, Kling 3.0, Kling 2.5 Turbo, Seedance 2.0, Veo 3.1, WAN 2.5, Sora 2, Minimax, UGC Factory
- **4 Production Workflows**:
  - Cinema Studio: 4K cinematic with ARRI/RED/IMAX camera simulation
  - Standard Video: Fast generation with multiple models for social/broadcast
  - UGC Builder: Photorealistic talking heads with native lip-sync
  - Storyboard: Multi-scene cinematography with consistency
- **Camera Control**: 70+ movement presets with up to 3 simultaneous stacking
- **Cinema Studio Hardware**:
  - 6 Camera Bodies (ARRI Alexa 35, RED V-Raptor, Sony Venice, IMAX, Panavision, Arriflex 16SR)
  - 11 Lens Options (Spherical, Anamorphic, Specialty)
  - Lighting Simulation (Key, fill, rim, practical lights)
- **Multi-Shot Mode**: 6 cuts in 12-15s clip without manual stitching
- **Reference Documentation**: 2,500+ lines across 6 guides (video-models, cinema-studio, camera-controls, ugc-guide, scene-template, stitching-guide)

### Agent: video-director (v1.0.0)
- **Full Production Orchestration**: Brief intake → planning → generation → stitching → delivery
- **Features**:
  - Intake & planning with clarifying questions (type, platform, duration, tone, brand)
  - Shot list creation using scene template format
  - Intelligent model selection decision tree
  - Generation across all 4 workflows via higgsfield-video skill
  - Multi-scene stitching (FFmpeg lossless concat)
  - Quality review checklist before delivery
  - Complete delivery package (video + shot list + prompts + notes)
  - Troubleshooting and re-take procedures
