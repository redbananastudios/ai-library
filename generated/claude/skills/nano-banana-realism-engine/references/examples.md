# Worked Examples

## Example 1: home services ad

**Input**
Create Facebook ad image concepts for a removals company targeting families moving house in the UK.

**Output pattern**
- 4 concepts
- one problem-aware hallway/box scene
- one outdoor van-loading documentary scene
- one aspirational settled-into-new-home scene
- one iPhone UGC testimonial-style scene

## Example 2: premium home goods

**Input**
Generate realistic prompts for an artisan kitchen brand launch.

**Output pattern**
- 3 concepts
- Fujifilm warm lifestyle and Sony A7III realism
- natural window light and golden hour
- clutter calibrated to “lived in but premium”
