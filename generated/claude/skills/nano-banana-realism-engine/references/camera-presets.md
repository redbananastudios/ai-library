# Camera Presets

Use one preset per concept. Choose the preset that best matches the audience, platform, and scene.

## Sony A7III realism
Best default for believable commercial photography and lifestyle imagery.

- Camera feel: full-frame mirrorless, natural digital realism
- Lens defaults:
  - 35mm for environmental scenes
  - 50mm for standard realism
  - 85mm for tighter portraits and selective focus
- Aperture range: f/1.8 to f/2.8
- Exposure feel: balanced, slightly naturalistic, not crushed
- Use when:
  - premium but believable lifestyle scenes
  - product-in-context ads
  - portraits that must not look stylized
- Typical language:
  - `shot on Sony A7III, 50mm lens, f/1.8, natural depth falloff, realistic sensor response`

## Canon R5 commercial
Best for cleaner product campaigns and polished but still human-made commercial images.

- Lens defaults:
  - 50mm macro-ish product scenes
  - 85mm portrait or detail shots
- Aperture range: f/2.0 to f/4.0
- Exposure feel: crisp subject, restrained highlights, controlled contrast
- Use when:
  - product-first ad creative
  - premium hero shots with real-world texture
  - fashion or grooming imagery with controlled detail
- Typical language:
  - `captured on Canon R5, 85mm lens, f/2.0, controlled highlight rolloff, true-to-life skin and material texture`

## iPhone UGC
Best for Meta, Facebook, Instagram, testimonial-style creatives, and “this looks like a real customer shot it” outputs.

- Camera feel: handheld smartphone capture
- Lens defaults:
  - native wide equivalent
  - slight perspective distortion is acceptable
- Exposure feel: auto-exposure, slightly uneven, sometimes mildly overexposed windows
- Imperfections:
  - small motion softness
  - noise in shadows
  - slightly inconsistent white balance
- Use when:
  - UGC
  - testimonials
  - family/home service ads
  - product in hand, mirror, kitchen, bathroom, car, street
- Typical language:
  - `shot on iPhone 14, handheld, quick candid capture, slight motion softness, mild sensor noise, realistic auto exposure`

## Fujifilm warm lifestyle
Best for editorial warmth, artisan products, interiors, and heritage or lifestyle brands.

- Camera feel: softer contrast, warm documentary tones
- Lens defaults:
  - 35mm or 56mm equivalent
- Aperture range: f/1.8 to f/2.8
- Exposure feel: slightly softer contrast, organic color rendering
- Use when:
  - home interiors
  - heritage kitchens
  - artisan or premium home goods
  - lifestyle editorial scenes
- Typical language:
  - `captured with a Fujifilm-style documentary look, soft warm color response, 35mm perspective, natural tonal rolloff`

## Studio-natural hybrid
Use only when the user explicitly wants a cleaner commercial look without falling into fake studio aesthetics.

- Camera feel: commercial but grounded
- Lens defaults:
  - 50mm or 85mm
- Aperture range: f/2.8 to f/5.6 depending on product depth
- Lighting expectation:
  - large diffused source
  - subtle fill
  - realistic tabletop or set reflections
- Use when:
  - ecommerce images that still need realism
  - product packs and ad variations requiring cleaner consistency
- Guardrails:
  - never describe hard rim lights, plastic skin, or perfectly dead backgrounds unless requested
