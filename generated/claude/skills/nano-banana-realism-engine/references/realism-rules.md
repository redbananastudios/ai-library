# Realism Rules

These rules are mandatory. They are the main anti-AI layer.

## Always add believable imperfection

Inject some of the following when suitable:

- slight framing imbalance
- minor lens softness at edges
- natural skin texture and pores
- a few flyaway hairs
- slight hand motion softness
- realistic garment creases
- tiny product scuffs, condensation, fingerprints, seam variation, or label wear
- uneven room brightness
- realistic shadow inconsistency from nearby objects
- mild sensor noise or grain
- natural clutter consistent with the setting

## Ban these failure modes

Never describe or imply:

- perfect facial symmetry
- plastic skin
- unreal catchlights
- hyper-detailed pores everywhere
- glossy CGI reflections
- perfectly centered compositions by default
- pristine empty rooms unless the brief requires it
- impossibly clean props
- impossible hand anatomy or impossible grip positions
- dramatic fantasy color grading
- surreal depth of field or physically implausible blur

## Human direction

Prefer:
- mid-action moments
- not always looking at the camera
- believable emotion over “model” energy
- posture that matches the activity
- clothing that fits the audience and context

Avoid:
- beauty-pageant posing
- arms hanging unnaturally
- fake laugh expressions
- stock-photo pointing gestures

## Product direction

Prefer:
- realistic scale
- believable reflections
- handling in context
- real environments
- matching props and surfaces

Avoid:
- floating product packs
- impossible liquid behavior
- over-sharpened labels
- melted or mirrored geometry
- fake macro detail that ignores lens behavior

## Environment direction

Prefer:
- lived-in rooms
- subtle clutter
- context that matches the customer
- specific local cues when the market is known

Avoid:
- showroom emptiness
- random luxury cues that contradict the audience
- inconsistent architecture or impossible room layouts

## Model-side negative controls

If the host image model supports negative prompts, bias against:
- cgi
- 3d render
- illustration
- anime
- wax skin
- extra fingers
- distorted hands
- duplicate items
- melted geometry
- overprocessed HDR
- text artifacts
- watermark
